Tom Merritt

Thomas Andrew Merritt (born June 28, 1970) is a
technology journalist, writer, and broadcaster best
known as the host of several podcasts. He is the former
co-host of Tech News Today on the TWiT.tv Network,
and was previously an Executive Editor for CNET
and developer and co-host of the daily podcast Buzz Out
Loud. He currently hosts Daily Tech News Show, Cordkillers,
and Sword and Laser, among other shows.
